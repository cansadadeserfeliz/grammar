Español vs Inglés

Articles in Spanish vs Articles in English

Indefinite Articles, Definite Articles, No Article (∅).

Inglés Español
Singular countable nouns
  1. Bye! Have a nice day.
  2. Do you need an umbrella?
  3. She never wears a hat.
  4. What a beautiful day!
  5. I've got a stomach ache.
  6. That's a nice table.
  7. When I was a child I used to be very shy.
  1. Adiós, ¡que tengas un buen día!
  2. ¿Necesitas una sombrilla?
  3. Ella nunca lleva ∅ sombrero.
  4. ¡Qué día ∅ tan bonito!
  5. Tengo ∅ dolor del estómago.
    (Más común: me duele el estómago.)
  6. Es una buena mesa.
Singular vs plural
  1. A dog is an animal.
  2. I'm an optimist.
  3. Tom's father is a doctor.
  4. Are you a good driver?
  5. Jane is a really nice person.
  6. What a lovely dress!
  7. Jack has a long nose.
  1. Dogs are ∅ animals.
  2. We're ∅ optimists.
  3. Most of my friends are ∅ students.
  4. Jane's parents are ∅ really nice people.
  5. What ∅ awful shoes!
  6. Jack has ∅ blue eyes.
Some = a number of / a few of / a pair of
  1. I've seen some good movies recently.
  2. Some friends of mine are coming to stay at the weekend.
  3. I need some new sunglasses. (=a new pair of sunglasses)
A/an and the: we a thinking of a specific thing
  1. Tim sat down on a chair.
  2. Tim sat down on the chair nearest the door.
  3. Can you turn off the light, please?
  4. I took a taxi to the station.
  5. I have to go to the bank and then I'm going to the post office.
  6. Is there a bank near here?
The: when there is only one of something
  • Have you ever crossed the equator?
  • What is the longest river in Europe?
  • Our apartment is on the tenth floor.
  • The internet changed the way we live.
  • The sun is a star.
(go to) the cinema, the theatre; the radio, ∅ television/TV
  1. I go to the cinema al lot, but I haven't been to the theatre for ages.
  2. I listen to the radio a lot.
  3. I watch ∅ television a lot.
  4. We watched it on ∅ TV.
  5. Can you turn of the TV, please? (the television set)
∅ breakfast/lunch/dinner
  1. What did you have for ∅ breakfast?
  2. We had ∅ lunch in a very nice restaurant.
  3. We had a very nice (big/wonderful/early) lunch.
∅ noun + number: Room 126, page 29, question 3, Gate 10
  1. Our train leaves from ∅ Platform 5.
  2. Do you have these shoes in ∅ size 43?
